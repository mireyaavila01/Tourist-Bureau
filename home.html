Welcome to Blue Ridge, Georgia!

Where Adventure Meets Relaxation

Nestled in the picturesque mountains of North Georgia, Blue Ridge is a charming town that offers a perfect blend of outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, and relaxation. Whether you're an adrenaline junkie, a history enthusiast, a wine connoisseur, or simply someone who loves to explore nature, Blue Ridge has something for everyone.

Whether you're embarking on a heart-pounding outdoor adventure, immersing yourself in local culture and history, or simply unwinding with a glass of fine wine against a backdrop of breathtaking scenery, Blue Ridge invites you to experience the best of North Georgia's mountain hospitality.

Activities We Offer:

Wine Tasting: Indulge in the region's finest wines at Blue Ridge's vineyards and wineries, where you can sample a variety of award-winning vintages while soaking in panoramic mountain views. Join a guided wine tour to explore multiple wineries and learn about the art of winemaking in North Georgia's burgeoning wine country.

Arts and Crafts: Unleash your creativity with painting activities offered in Blue Ridge. Whether you're a novice or an experienced artist, immerse yourself in the vibrant local arts scene with painting workshops, where you can learn new techniques and create your own masterpiece inspired by the beauty of the surrounding mountains and countryside.

Adventurous River Rafting and Hot Air Balloon Rides: Thrill-seekers can embark on a whitewater rafting adventure down the wild and scenic Ocoee River, just a short drive from Blue Ridge. Navigate through exhilarating rapids and take in breathtaking views of the surrounding wilderness. Experience the ultimate adventure with a hot air balloon ride over the Blue Ridge Mountains. Drift serenely above the picturesque landscape as the sunrise bathes the mountains in golden light, offering breathtaking views that will leave you in awe.

Museums: Delve into the rich history and heritage of Blue Ridge at their many museums. Explore exhibits showcasing the town's past, from its early days as a thriving mining and railroad hub to its evolution into a beloved mountain getaway.Discover exhibits highlighting the area's ecological significance, outdoor recreation opportunities.

Other Options: Some other options that are a unique experience include getting a Firefighter Truck Ride and Volunteeting in the community. Hop aboard a real firefighter truck and embark on an exhilarating ride through the town, sirens wailing and lights flashing. Experience the rush of adrenaline as you cruise through the streets like a true hero, gaining insight into the daily life of our courageous firefighters.Make a difference in the lives of others by volunteering in the Blue Ridge community. Whether it's lending a helping hand at a local soup kitchen, participating in a neighborhood cleanup initiative, or assisting with community events, there are countless opportunities to give back and strengthen the bonds that unite us.
